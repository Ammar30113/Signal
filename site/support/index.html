Signal Support

Need help with Signal? Contact the support team by email.

Email: supportsignalteam@gmail.com

What to include

  • Your device model and operating system version.
  • What you were trying to do.
  • What happened instead.
  • Whether the issue happens every time or only sometimes.

Privacy note

Signal is local-first. We cannot see your check-ins, SOS sessions, slip reviews, or exported data unless you choose to share them. Do not send sensitive personal notes unless you intentionally want support to review them.

Crisis note

Signal is not a crisis service, therapy, or medical care. If you are in immediate danger or experiencing a mental health crisis, contact local emergency services or a licensed professional.
