# Signal Privacy Policy

_Last updated: June 5, 2026_

Signal is a private, local-first urge-interruption app. This policy explains, in plain language, what data Signal handles and how.

## The short version

- Signal stores your data **only on your device.**
- Signal has **no account, no login, and no server.** Nothing you log is uploaded to us.
- Signal contains **no analytics, no advertising, and no third-party trackers.**
- You can **export or permanently delete** your data at any time from the Privacy screen.

## What data Signal stores

Signal lets you log information to help you notice and interrupt urges, including:

- Check-ins (mood, urge intensity, triggers, and related prompts)
- SOS / intervention sessions (timer use, chosen action, before/after intensity, and your written reflection)
- Slip reviews (your notes about what happened and what would help next time)
- App settings (such as protocol duration and whether App Lock is on)

All of this is stored locally on your device using on-device storage. It is **not** transmitted to Signal or to any third party.

## How your data is used

Your data is used solely on your device to:

- Show your current state, risk level, and trends
- Build your local pattern map (top triggers, danger windows, redirects that worked)

There is no remote processing. We never see your entries.

## Sharing and disclosure

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal data, because we never receive it. The only time data leaves your device is when **you** choose to use the **Export** feature, which produces a file you control and can share wherever you decide. Anything you share that way is your responsibility.

## App Lock

You can enable App Lock to require Face ID, Touch ID, or your device passcode before opening Signal. Authentication is performed by your device's operating system; Signal never receives your biometric data.

## Data retention and deletion

Your data remains on your device until you delete it. Use **Delete local data** on the Privacy screen to remove your check-ins, SOS sessions, slip reviews, and settings. Deleting the app from your device also removes its local data.

## Children

Signal is intended for adults (rated 17+) and is not directed to children.
